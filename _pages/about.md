Hi there! I am an undergraduate student in Electronic Information and Technology at **Tsinghua University** (expected graduation in 2027), selected for the highly competitive **"AI + System" Talent Program** (Top 30%). Currently, I am a visiting exchange student at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), **Nanyang Technological University (NTU)** in Singapore.

My research broadly focuses on the intersection of **Large Language Models (LLMs), Multimodal AI, Graph Learning, and Combinatorial Optimization**. I am particularly interested in building efficient, scalable, and memory-optimized AI systems for dynamic real-world environments.

Currently, I am working as a intern at **MMLab@NTU** under the supervision of [Prof. Ziwei Liu](https://liuziwei7.github.io/), focusing on memory compression strategies for Large Video Models. Previously, I worked on LLM-driven dynamic network routing and graph neural networks under the guidance of [Prof. Quanming Yao](https://lars-group.github.io/) at Tsinghua.
