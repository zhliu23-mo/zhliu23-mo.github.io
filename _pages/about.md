Hi there! I am **Zhongheng Liu**, an undergraduate student in Electronic Information and Technology at **Tsinghua University** (expected graduation in June 2027), selected for the **"AI + System" Talent Program**.

I recently completed an exchange semester at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), **Nanyang Technological University (NTU)** in Singapore. I am currently working on memory compression for Large Video Models with **MMLab@NTU** under the supervision of [Prof. Ziwei Liu](https://liuziwei7.github.io/). In Summer 2026, I will join **HKUST** for a summer research program.

My research broadly centers on efficient and adaptive AI systems, especially **LLM agents, multimodal AI, graph learning, reinforcement learning, and combinatorial optimization**. Previously, I worked with [Prof. Quanming Yao](https://lars-group.github.io/) at Tsinghua on LLM-based reflective evolution for dynamic network routing, contributing to **NeRM-Net** and **LERNA-Flow**.

You can find my full CV here: [Download CV](/files/CV_ZhonghengLiu.pdf).
